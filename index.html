About Me

Outside of school, tech, and career stuff, I'm basically fueled by curiosity, adrenaline, and good food.

I love experiences that make me feel alive — I'm the kind of person who'd rather jump out of a plane than watch one take off. I'm drawn to anything intense, risky, or story-worthy, and I'm always looking for my next "that was insane" moment. On my bucket list: skydiving, riding a motorcycle, and even going on a boar hunt.

I'm also a huge food person — not cooking it, just eating it. I'll happily travel across a city for a place everyone swears is "worth it." Half my memories are tied to meals, late-night food runs, or arguing about what the best way to eat chicken is (a debate I take way too seriously).

Creatively, I enjoy making people laugh. I like making ridiculous videos, absurd concepts, and running jokes that somehow become real traditions. Humor for me is half entertainment and half social experiment.

I'm fascinated by history, politics, and why people behave the way they do — not academically, but conversationally. I like debates, hypotheticals, and overthinking random topics at 2am.

I also enjoy games — not just playing them but thinking about systems, mechanics, and how small rules create big experiences. I like breaking things down mentally: why something is fun, why people care, and why certain ideas stick.

Overall, I like experiences > possessions, stories > comfort, and memorable moments > safe ones.
