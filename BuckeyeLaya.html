About Buckeye Laya

Buckeye Laya is a premier South Asian a cappella competition that brings together talented vocal groups from across the nation to showcase their unique blend of Eastern and Western musical traditions.

Celebrating Cultural Harmony Through Music

Founded at The Ohio State University, Buckeye Laya has grown into a nationally recognized event that celebrates the rich musical traditions of South Asia while embracing contemporary Western influences.

As an official "bid competition" certified by the Association of South-Asian A Cappella (ASA), top-performing teams earn points toward qualifying for the national championship, All-American Awaaz.

The Competition

Buckeye Laya brings together the most talented collegiate South Asian a cappella groups for a day of incredible performances, cultural celebration, and musical excellence.

Competition Structure

Teams compete in a carefully judged format that evaluates musicality, creativity, and cultural authenticity.

  • 12-minute performance sets
  • Judged by industry professionals
  • ASA bid points awarded
